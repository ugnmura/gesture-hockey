# Gesture Hockey

Play now on https://ugnmura.github.io/gesture-hockey/!
Simple 2 Player Air Hockey Game

## How to play

- Enable camera access when prompted. The game detects both players' hands using the live video feed.
- Two players should stand or sit on opposite sides of the screen Each player controls a paddle using their hands.
- Keep both hands visible to the camera. Move your hands forward, backward, and sideways to hit the puck and defend your goal.
- The first player to 10 points wins the match.
- For best tracking: use good lighting, avoid fast sudden movements, and keep the background simple.

## Techstack

- next.js
- mediapipe (handtracking)
- pixi.js (graphics)
- matter.js (physics)
